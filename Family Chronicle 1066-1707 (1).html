The Progenitors

It begins in the fog of war, upon the fields where fates were decided by steel and blood. Our ancestors, borne of Norman stock and Saxon resilience, first appear in the dusty rolls of the Domesday Book. Through centuries of upheaval, plague, and renaissance, the line remained unbroken—a testament to the enduring spirit of the family name. This chronicle serves not merely as a record of names and dates, but as a tapestry woven from the lives of those who walked before us.
